Know The Different Types Of Medical Students:

Which One Is Your Type?

You know what’s interesting about studying medicine; it is the variety of people you come across. Yes, besides those anatomy sessions and different medical courses, this is the thing which makes the medical training a worthwhile experience for most aspiring doctors. Believe it or not but medical students are a completely different set of people. Often thought to be the most studious group, they are actually more than that. You will find a funny mixture of personalities in each of them and that’s what makes this long period of medical study a fun experience. So if you were of the idea that every other student in a medical college is a bookworm and has no life, then this post is for you.

Whether you are studying mbbs abroad or taking it up in your own country, you will find a lot of different people during your course of medical education. Here’s the list that you will usually meet:

• The Friend Of All: This medical student is the most familiar type, who will immediately get comfortable around a group of people. He/She will laugh at your jokes, sneak out of the hospital early with you and will help you whenever you want. In short, a total friendly type, the one who will easily adjust in your gang.

• The Escaper: To be more professional, you can call him/her an escapologist. He/She is one of those kinds who will always be occupied and will be the busiest either in some lecture, lab, conference, important meeting or a personal work. This medical student will always leave early and show up late most of times. In any way, he/she will find out the perfect way to escape the medical training every time.

• The Overly Sweet Ones: Can be called as the fluffer, this medical student is really ambitious and is eager to go into the field in which you are currently rotating. He/She will make every effort to set a good impression while studying medicine so that he/she can come into limelight and bag that position. He/she will do buttering to such an extent that you won’t be able to ignore, even if you wanted to.

• The Question Machine: This is one of the most common types that you will come across in your medical study. These folks are always found with pen and paper in hand and leave no opportunity to ask questions and interrogate about the specific condition. They have a lot of questions to ask.

• The Go-getter: You can call him/her a gunner as well as this one manages to peek into all the cool surgeries by following around the attending physician. He/she is that restless personality who refuses to go home and would always look for someone to help him/her out. In other words, he/she will advance himself at the expense of others.

• The Future: the most serious category of medical students, these folks are counted as the good students who are really decent and are here to study. Everyone wants them in their group but they make their own choices and lead others.

Without a doubt, your medical college has them all. But, which one are you?
