Achieving My Dream To Be A Doctor

I always wanted to become a doctor. Maybe because it was of my compassion towards the sick or to see everyone around me happy that led me to pursue the career of becoming a professional medical practitioner, but it was the best decision in my life. I was always supported in this decision of going for medical study by my parents. So, I set myself to prepare for MBBS admission in a reputed medical college. While striving for the same, I observed that the cost of medical study in the medical colleges in India was comparatively higher than those in the Philippines. So, I started looking out for options to study MBBS abroad. There are several top-rated medical colleges in Philippines that offer MBBS admission at highly affordable fees.

Lyceum-Northwestern University –the College of My Dreams

As I was doing my research for the best MBBS colleges in Philippines, I came across Lyceum-Northwestern University. Established in 1969, this is a private university offering a myriad of world-class medical courses to the students. Lyceum-Northwestern University is amongst the top medical colleges in Philippines. It is one of the MCI approved medical colleges in Philippines and has been producing the best in-class group of professionals every year through its high success rate.

The best thing about getting an MBBS admission in this medical college is that there is no entrance test required. You can get MBBS admission on the basis of your merit in the medical field. I was fortunate to be a part of this world-class medical institute to pursue my MBBS degree and thus, become a doctor.
